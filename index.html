Meet the Leadership

Zulfiya Forsythe

CEO of Omadli Group

Zulfiya Forsythe is a seasoned CPA and Data Analyst who had over a decade of experience in the healthcare, hospitality, and real estate industries before she founded The Omadli Group. Zulfiya began her data analytics journey as a business system analyst, determined to streamline lengthy, repetitive monthly processes into efficient reporting systems. She was eager to develop skills that would enable her to extract, manipulate, and analyze data, and transform it into meaningful storytelling.

Coming from a financial background, Zulfiya understood the importance of accuracy and time management in the marathon stretch of monthly close procedures. This experience served as the fuel to develop solutions that saved time, while also providing more accurate data with a focus on analysis and decision-making.

Fast-forward to today, Zulfiya now leads her own full-service data analytics firm, leveraging her diverse industry experience to provide clients with customized solutions that optimize their operations, improve performance, and achieve their goals. Her commitment to providing clients with valuable insights and data-driven solutions has made her a sought-after expert in the industry.
